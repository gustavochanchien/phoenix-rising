About Phoenix Rising
Experienced RC balloon pilots powering proven education programs.

Phoenix Rising brings together people of all ages to marvel at the amazing physics and history behind ballooning in New Mexico and enjoy and learn about the craft of R/C ballooning. We fly mostly in the fall and winter months when the air is colder and provides more lift. Our team members have years of experience flying for educational programs and demos at the Anderson Abruzzo Hot Air Balloon Museum. We follow proper safety protocol, and love to teach others about hot air balloons and let them experience them up close. We also love sharing our knowledge on R/C ballooning how we weave our own R/C gondolas, our electronics and telemetry systems, and more!

Education & Innovation
RC Balloon programs that plug directly into your curriculum & events.

Our RC balloons are more than display pieces—they are mobile, repeatable education platforms. We collaborate with museums, schools, nonprofits, and festivals to deliver structured experiences that blend live flight, storytelling, and hands-on STEAM.

  • Field Trips – “We Are a Balloon Crew”
    Students act as the ground crew, learn each part of the balloon, assist with inflation, launch, and pack-up, and experience burners and controls up close.
  • Science in the Sky
    Live science demonstrations plus RC balloon flights for elementary & middle school groups, tying in weather, wind, solar power, flight, astronomy, and New Mexico science careers.
  • Montgolfier Days
    Celebrations of the first hot air balloon flights where fifth graders launch their own tissue balloons, supported by RC balloon demos and teacher training.
  • Ballooning for All – Accessibility Programs
    Inclusive balloon rallies designed for participants with special needs, where RC balloons extend the experience when full-size balloons can’t fly.
  • Balloon Fiesta & Major Festivals
    Daily RC balloon demonstrations for international visitors, offering an up-close balloon experience even outside ideal flight windows.
  • Stories in the Sky
    Early childhood literacy and cultural programs enhanced with periodic RC balloon flights that connect books, art, and science.
